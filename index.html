Download MetaMask Extension: Chrome Setup Tutorial

Detailed how-to guide showing how to download the MetaMask extension on Chrome, install it properly, and configure basic security settings with ease.

Complete Installation Guide

1Open Google Chrome Browser

Open Google Chrome on your computer. For the best performance and security, make sure you are using the latest version of Chrome. To check for updates, click the three dots in the top-right corner, select “Help” → “About Google Chrome,” and allow Chrome to update and restart if needed.

2Visit the Chrome Web Store

Go to the official Chrome Web Store by typing chrome.google.com/webstore into your address bar, or search for “MetaMask Chrome extension” using your preferred search engine and follow the result that leads to the Chrome Web Store.

Important: Always confirm that the URL starts with https://chrome.google.com/. Installing extensions from unofficial websites can expose you to fake or malicious versions of MetaMask.

3Search for MetaMask

In the Chrome Web Store search bar, type “MetaMask” and press Enter. Identify the official MetaMask extension by checking for all of the following:

  • The recognizable orange MetaMask fox logo
  • Developer listed as “metamask.io” or “https://metamask.io”
  • Millions of users and a strong overall rating from real reviews
  • A verified badge indicating that it is the official, trusted extension

4Install the Extension

Click the official MetaMask extension result, then select the “Add to Chrome” button. A confirmation pop-up from Chrome will appear, asking you to approve the installation.

Carefully review the permissions requested by MetaMask (for example, the ability to read and change data on the websites you visit). If everything looks correct and expected, click “Add extension” to continue with the installation.

5Wait for Installation to Complete

Chrome will automatically download and install the MetaMask extension. This usually takes only a few seconds. When it’s finished, you will see the MetaMask fox icon appear in the Chrome toolbar at the top-right corner of your browser window.

If you don’t see the icon, click the puzzle piece (Extensions) icon in the toolbar, find MetaMask in the list, and click the pin icon next to it so that MetaMask is always visible and easy to access.

6Set Up Your Wallet

Click the MetaMask icon in your toolbar to launch the setup flow. You will see two main options:

  • Create a Wallet: Choose this if you are new to MetaMask and want to generate a brand-new wallet on this device.
  • Import Wallet: Select this if you already have a MetaMask wallet and you want to restore it using your existing Secret Recovery Phrase.

7Create a Strong Password

If you create a new wallet, MetaMask will first ask you to set a password. This password protects your wallet on this specific device. Follow these best practices:

  • Use at least 8–12 characters for better protection
  • Combine uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Include numbers and special characters
  • Avoid using names, birthdays, or any easily guessed information

8Secure Your Recovery Phrase

MetaMask will then display your Secret Recovery Phrase (also called a seed phrase), which is a list of 12 words. This is the most critical security element of your wallet:

  • Write down all 12 words in the exact order shown on the screen.
  • Store the phrase in a safe, offline place (for example, on paper kept in a secure location).
  • Do not save screenshots, photos, or digital copies in email, cloud storage, or messaging apps.
  • Never share your recovery phrase with anyone—MetaMask support or any legitimate service will never ask for it.

Warning: Anyone who gains access to your recovery phrase can take full control of your wallet and your funds. Treat it with the same or greater level of care as your bank PIN or online banking credentials.

9Confirm Your Recovery Phrase

MetaMask will ask you to confirm your Secret Recovery Phrase by selecting the words in the correct order. This step ensures that you have saved the phrase accurately and can restore your wallet in the future if needed. Complete this verification slowly and double-check each word before proceeding.

10Start Using MetaMask

Once the setup is complete, your MetaMask wallet is ready to use. From your browser, you can now:

  • Send, receive, and store Ethereum and compatible ERC-20 tokens
  • Connect your wallet to decentralized applications (dApps)
  • Interact with DeFi protocols such as lending, staking, and swapping
  • Purchase and manage NFTs and other digital collectibles
  • Create and manage multiple accounts within the same MetaMask wallet

Important Security Tips

  • Never share your Secret Recovery Phrase with anyone, including MetaMask support, friends, or websites.
  • Watch out for phishing attempts – always check website URLs, avoid clicking suspicious links, and only connect your wallet on trusted, verified platforms.
  • Use a hardware wallet together with MetaMask if you hold large amounts of cryptocurrency, for an extra layer of protection.
  • Keep your browser and operating system updated and enable built-in security features such as safe browsing and malware protection.
  • Double-check wallet addresses before sending any transaction. Consider using small test transactions when sending to a new address for the first time.
  • Be cautious with new dApps and features and start with small amounts until you fully understand how a protocol or website works.
